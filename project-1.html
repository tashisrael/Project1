Training Overview

During my training at the Uganda Institute of Banking sessions tailored for schools and communities, I delved deep into financial literacy, covering a comprehensive range of topics designed to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills to manage their finances well. The sessions spanned around personal financial management (handling budgeting, record keeping and tracking expenses), savings, investment, loans management, digital literacy and planning for old age and retirement. We also explored various guiding principles like the 50-30-20 budgeting rule in a bid to effectively plan for their money well.

In addition, Frugal living tips addressing resource wastage in all aspects were explored and shared with the participants.
