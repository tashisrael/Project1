Training Overview

During the PDM beneficiary empowerement sessions in Tororo covering 3 sub counties, it was acknoledged that debt remains neccessary especially for reasons such as recapitalization, unexpected emmergencies and many others. However the aspect of debt handling and management remains significant starting with the reasons for which we borrow. The beneficiaries were enlightened on debt management techniques such as making a list of all debts, structuring debt repayment as per the income cycle etc.

Emphasis was put on the need for an emmergency fund as most of the reasons they borrow for culminate from lack of an emmergency fund.
