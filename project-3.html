Training Overview

Communities like churches, markets and savings groups are ideal points of sustainanble transformation that overtime lead to economic development. During these sessions, the integration of financial education and spiritual life was emphasized as a starting point to financial freedom. Principles such as goal setting, budgeting, savings, debt repayment and investment were discussed. Budgeting was emphasized as a major tool in planning for money well. The principle of tithe (Malachi 3:10) was emphasized as a number one item on the budget followed by saving, loan repayment, needs and wants.

"Do not save what is left after spending, but spend what is left after saving." - Warren Buffett
